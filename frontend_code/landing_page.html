Subscription management FAQs

What is subscription management, and why is it important for businesses?
Subscription management is the process of handling the entire lifecycle of a customer's subscription. It's crucial for maintaining recurring revenue streams, reducing churn, and automating billing processes to scale efficiently.
What is subscription software?
Subscription software automates the recurring billing process, manages customer plans, handles dunning (failed payments), and provides analytics on revenue metrics.
How can I manage my subscriptions?
You can manage subscriptions through our dashboard or API. We provide tools for creating plans, modifying existing subscriptions, and handling cancellations or upgrades seamlessly.
What is Billing?
Billing is our comprehensive solution for invoicing and subscription management. It sits on top of our payments infrastructure to help you collect revenue efficiently.
